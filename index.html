Pori Moni to feature in Aronno Anwar’s first film

Pori Moni has signed a movie with noted television director Aronno Anwar for his first directorial film "Maa". The deal was signed yesterday at the actress' house. Based on a true story, the movie centers around the story of a mother as she struggles with the emotions of losing her seven-month-old baby who was declared dead in 1971. Here the central character of the mother will be played by "Bishwoshundori" actress Pori Moni.
